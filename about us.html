ABOUT ELLS GARDEN
Ells Garden is a family-owned event garden in Boatle established in 2023. It was created from a shared family vision to provide a beautiful and relaxing space for photoshoots, weddings, celebrations, and other gatherings. Our objective is to grow bigger, possibly have other places in Botswana, while creating memorable experiences for every guest.
